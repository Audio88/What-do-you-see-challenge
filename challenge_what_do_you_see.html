house_income = df['LMED']
house_income = house_income.dropna()

house_income_adj = df['APLMED']
house_income_adj = house_income_adj.dropna()

plt.figure(figsize=(10, 5))

plt.subplot(1, 2, 1)
plt.title('Before')
plt.boxplot(house_income)
plt.ylabel('Income')

plt.subplot(1, 2, 2)
plt.title('After')
plt.boxplot(house_income_adj)
plt.ylabel('Income')

plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()

Two boxplots showing income before and after being adjusted by standard HUD adjustment factors. On average incomes come down, but a few outliers earn more.
